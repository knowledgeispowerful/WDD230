Target Audience

Our chamber of commerce caters to the following target audience:

  • Local business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Professionals seeking networking opportunities
  • Community members interested in supporting local businesses

Personas

Persona 1: Emily Sanders

Emily is a small business owner running a boutique clothing store. She is looking for ways to connect with other local business owners and promote her store to a wider audience.

Persona 2: David Johnson

David is a young professional who recently moved to the area. He is interested in networking events and mentorship opportunities to expand his professional connections and grow his career.

Scenarios

  1. Emily wants to attend a chamber networking event to meet other business owners and potentially collaborate on marketing initiatives.
  2. David is looking for a mentor in the industry and wants to find out if the chamber offers a mentorship program.
  3. A local resident wants to find a directory of all the chamber member businesses to support local shopping.
  4. Emily is interested in the chamber's resources for improving social media marketing strategies.
  5. David wants to learn about upcoming workshops or seminars organized by the chamber that can help him enhance his professional skills.
