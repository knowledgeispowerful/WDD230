The color scheme for our chamber website is based on shades of green. The primary color is #007BFF (royal blue), with complementary shades used for accents and backgrounds.

Typography:

We have chosen the "Roboto" font for our website. It offers a clean and modern look, ensuring readability across different devices. The main body text will be set in 16px Roboto, while headings will be in various weights for visual hierarchy.
